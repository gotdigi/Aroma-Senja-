Our Story

Born from the hour
when light turns gold.

"Senja" means dusk — that brief, unrepeatable hour when the sky softens. We opened Aroma Senja in 2018 to bottle that feeling: a slow cup, a low hum of conversation, and beans roasted just enough to taste like home.

Every batch is sourced from small Indonesian growers in Gayo, Toraja, and Flores, roasted in-house weekly, and brewed by hand — never rushed, never over-extracted.
